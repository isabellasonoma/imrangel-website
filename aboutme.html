    About Me

    I was raised on the property of my family restaurant & inn, on the Sonoma Coast in Northern California. I was an active participant in the family business and filled nearly every role through the years. I grew up cycling, hiking, and kayaking, taking advantage of the gorgeous nature around me. My current favorites are rock climbing, reading, film photography, and sailing.

    I'm currently a senior at USC studying International Business and Mathematics. In the world of information we live in, I'm excited about data science because it allows us to extract insights from data that's already being collected. I love the interdisciplinary nature of my studies, and I'm excited to continue learning.
